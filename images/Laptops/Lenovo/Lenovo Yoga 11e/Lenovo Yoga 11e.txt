OVERVIEW
Lenovo ThinkPad Yoga 11E is a laptop with a 11.60-inch display that has a resolution of 1366x768 pixels. It is powered by a Celeron Dual Core processor and it comes with 4GB of RAM. The Lenovo ThinkPad Yoga 11E packs 300GB of HDD storage.
Graphics are powered by Intel HD Graphics 520. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 ac, Bluetooth.
